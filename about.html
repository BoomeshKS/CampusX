Our Story

CampusX was born with a vision: to bridge the gap between college education and real-world entrepreneurship. Many students have brilliant ideas but lack the right platform, mentorship, and opportunities. That’s where CampusX steps in — turning college campuses into startup launchpads.

  • Empowering innovative student ideas.
  • Providing mentorship and resources.
  • Transforming campuses into startup hubs.

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission:

To empower 1,000 student startups by 2030 through knowledge, collaboration, and support.

Vision:
