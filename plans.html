FAQ’s

Got Membership Questions? We Have Answers!

Unlock your entrepreneurial potential with CampusX membership benefits. Join our vibrant community to network, learn, and grow with exclusive opportunities tailored for aspiring entrepreneurs and innovators.

Join Now
Is the membership fee refundable?

No, the membership fee is non-refundable, but it provides year-long benefits and exclusive opportunities.

Are events included in the membership?

Yes, members enjoy 50% off on all CampusX events, meetups, hackathons, and conferences.

Do I need to be a startup founder to join?

Not at all. Membership is open to students, aspiring entrepreneurs, innovators, and anyone interested in startups.

What if I don’t have a startup idea yet?

No problem. You can learn, network, and build skills through workshops, masterclasses, and collaborations until you’re ready to start.

How long is the membership valid?

Your membership is valid for a year from the date of registration.

Can I renew my membership?

Yes, memberships can be renewed annually to continue enjoying all benefits.

Can I collaborate with other members on projects?

Yes! CampusX provides a community platform and co-working space where members can collaborate on ideas and projects.

Do members get certificates?

Yes, members will receive certificates for workshops, hackathons, and selected masterclasses they participate in.

Can non-members attend CampusX events?

Yes, non-members can attend, but they won’t receive discounts, mentorship, or exclusive member benefits.

Can I volunteer or be part of the CampusX organizing team?

Yes! Members get priority in volunteering opportunities, event organization, and leadership roles.

Do I need to be from a particular college or city to join?

No. CampusX is an international-level community, open to students and aspiring entrepreneurs from anywhere in India.

Will CampusX help me get funding for my startup?

Yes, CampusX provides guidance on funding schemes, connects with investors, and helps you prepare for funding opportunities.

Can working professionals also join CampusX?

Yes, though CampusX primarily focuses on students, early-stage entrepreneurs and professionals are also welcome to join.
